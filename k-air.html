A New History of Korean Air Begins

January 18, 2018 Incheon International Airport

Terminal 2 Relocation

------------

Since January 18, 2018, Korean Air operates in Ternimal 2 of Incheon Airport.

Please use the Korean Air check-in counters at Ternimal 2 if you are departing from Incheon Airport.

If you're flying on a codeshare flight, please double check the operating carrier's ternimal before departure.

Transportation Information

Public Transportation (Limousine/Railway)

Please confirm Terminal 2 stop before getting off. KAL limousine stops first at Terminal 2.

Driving to the Airport

Follow the signs to Terminal 2 and enter the new road.
